Relationship Guide

How Does this whole thing work?

Stardew Valley has a lot of different things to do in the game. Farming, obviously, is the game's core mechanic, but there is also game mechanics for mining, combat, friendships, and even dating and marriage! This guide is here to help you find what items to give to each villager in order to increase your friendship level. The rewards for this effort, outside of being the most popular farmer in town, are new recipes, resources, and good old fashioned coin.

Freindship Level

Let's start out with how friendships work. There is a number that is associated with your friendship with a NPC. It starts at zero and will increase through interactions with the character. The most common and effective interaction for increasing your friendship level is to give that person a gift. Each character has gifts that they love, like, are neutral towards, dislike and hate. To make things a little more intersting, all of these lists are different for each character.

There are lists of items that are universal for each category, but they include exceptions too.

Universal Loves

  • Golden Pumpkin
  • Magic Rock Candy
  • Pearl
  • Prismatic Shard
  • Rabbit's Foot

Universal Loves Exceptions

  • Haley hates Prismatic Shards
  • Penny hates Rabbit's Foot

Universal Likes

  • All Artisan Goods Expect for Oil, which is disliked, and Void Mayonaise, which is hated
