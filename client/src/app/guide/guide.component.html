Relationship Guide

Background

Stardew Valley has a lot of different things to do in the game. Farming, obviously, is the game's core mechanic, but there is also game mechanics for mining, combat, friendships, and even dating and marriage! This guide is here to help you find what items to give to each villager in order to increase your friendship level. The rewards for this effort, outside of being the most popular farmer in town, are new recipes, resources, and good old fashioned coin.

Friendship Level

Let's start out with how friendships work. There is a number that is associated with the friendship between you and a NPC. It starts at zero and will increase through interactions with the character. This number is displayed to you as a heart meter between 0 and 10 hearts. A 'Marriable' NPC's limit without dating them is 8 hearts. 'Non-Marriable' NPCs go up to 10. The most common and effective interaction for increasing your friendship level is to give that person a gift. Each character has gifts that they love, like, are neutral towards, dislike or hate. To make things a little more interesting, all of these lists are different for each character.

What should I do?

There are some things, such as Prismatic Shards, which are universally loved. Except by Haley, who hates them. And they are super rare, and they are super valuable. So you probably won't be giving them away early on in your playthrough. That is pretty much how these universal lists work. What our aim is here on this site it to allow you to decide which items to grow/gather for use as gifts that are somewhat easy to come by if you plan ahead.

Basic Strategy

Do you rememeber me mentioning that there is a number associated with each relationship that you have with NPCs throughout the game? Well that number can be anywhere between 0 and 2749. There are a lot of different things you can do to make that number go up or down. Simply interacting with the NPC will raise that number by 20 points each day, unless they are in the middle of some activity. Then it will only increase by 10. See what I mean by the exceptions thing. It will always be something, unless it is not. If you are a min/max type player who likes to know every single way to gain advantages over everything in a game, then you should check out the Stardew Valley Wiki for all of the different ifs, ands, and buts that make up relationships in the game.


I have a simple approach to the relationship problem that seems to work pretty well. This approach takes advantage of two things. 1. Every NPC has a birthday. 2. Every NPC likes tree fruit. I would suggest that when Demetrius shows up at your farm looking to set up an experiment in the caves, select the fruit bat option over the mushroom one. This will give you some early access to tree fruits. Pierre sells tree fruit saplings at his shop, which are relativiely expensive considering that it will take you awhile to make your money back from selling the fruit and it will take a whole season for the fruit tree to grow. It still would be a good idea to grab a peach or orange tree as soon as you can spare the gold for one and place it on your farm. Gus loves oranges, and Robin loves peaches. Everyone else likes both. Once you have the green house unlocked either through the community center or by selling your soul at Jojo Mart, you can plant all six fruit trees inside against the walls. Grow trees, gather fruits, buy friendships.


The most important part of this whole scheme is to rememeber birthdays. There is a calander outside of Pierre's shop which shows upcoming ones. I recommend buying one from Robin, because it can be annoying to travel all the way to town and see that you need to go back to the farm to grab a gift for someone. For birthdays escpecially, it makes a big difference to gift a NPC something that they love. The way the math works out, the difference amounts to just under a whole heart. It's kind of a big deal.


In summary, find out what each NPC loves to get as a gift and make sure you give them it on their birthday. The rest of the time, make a gift run with a lot of tree fruits as you make your way through town to do other things. Before long, you will be the most popular farmer in the land. Have fun!
